Asset management is the process of tracking and managing an organization's physical and digital assets, such as hardware, software, and equipment. It involves maintaining records of who is assigned specific equipment, the condition of each asset, and ensuring that assets are up-to-date and properly maintained. This helps in optimizing asset usage, improving security, and ensuring compliance with regulations.
